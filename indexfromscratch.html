Motivational Speech

I am an international student living in the US.

 

As an international student living in the United States, there are many experiences and challenges that are unique to this situation,

First and foremost, there is the cultural adjustment that comes with moving to a new country. The United States is a melting pot of cultures, and each region has its own unique customs and traditions. As an international student, you may find yourself needing to adapt to a new way of life, including food, language, social norms, and even weather.

One of the biggest challenges for international students is homesickness. Being away from your family and friends for an extended period can be difficult, especially if you're not used to living alone. This can be exacerbated by the fact that many international students come from cultures where family is highly valued and staying connected with them is a priority.
